==========================================

KEY DESIGN DECISIONS
- Two-tier topology: 9 leaf nodes + 3 hub nodes + 1 base station
- In-blade radio: Si4463 @ 915 MHz, +13 dBm (sub-GHz for blade penetration)
- Outbound (hub-to-base): Wi-Fi HaLow with FEM @ +21 dBm
- MCU: STM32U585 (lowest sleep current, hardware FPU for OPUS encoding)
- Audio compression: OPUS 22:1 (44.1 kHz → 4 kbps)
- Cycle: 30 s sample every 10 minutes (5% duty cycle)

VERIFIED DATASHEET NUMBERS (used in this design)
- Si4463 @ +13 dBm: 44.5 mA TX, 13.7 mA RX, -103 dBm sensitivity, 500 kbps
  Source: Silicon Labs Si4464/63/61/60 Rev 1.4 (Oct 2024)
- STM32U585: 19 µA/MHz active, 0.3 µA Stop2 sleep
  Source: ST DS13086
- nRF5340: 6.40 mA TX @ 0 dBm, 6.26 mA RX, -95 dBm @ 1M PHY
  Source: Nordic Product Specification v1.5
- MM6108 HaLow (+ FEM module): +21 dBm output, ~320 mA TX
  Source: Morse Micro MF08651-US Data Sheet v1.1

ENERGY BUDGET PER LEAF (datasheet-verified, 10 min cycle, 30 s sample, OPUS, 20°C)
  Sleep      :   0.3 µA × 544 s =   0.6 mJ  (0.0%)
  Audio + IMU:   3.0 mA ×  30 s = 333.1 mJ  (21.2%)
  Compression:   6.1 mA ×  21 s = 484.2 mJ  (30.8%)
  Radio TX   :  46.5 mA × 4.3 s = 740.4 mJ  (47.1%)
  RX ACK     :  15.7 mA × 0.2 s =  11.6 mJ  (0.7%)
  Other      :                  =   3.2 mJ  (0.2%)
  -------------------------------------------------
  TOTAL                         =  1,573 mJ per cycle
  
  Energy/day: 227 J
  Battery life on 20 Ah pack: 3.22 years
  Ah needed for 1-year target: 6.2 Ah (use 10–20 Ah for safety margin)

DATA FLOW (per 10-min cycle)
  T=0       Leaf nodes wake, sample 30 s audio + IMU
  T=30      Compress with OPUS in parallel on each leaf
  T=51      TDMA TX windows on Si4463 (3 leaves × ~5 s)
  T=66      Hub aggregates 3 streams (~390 KB total)
  T=70      Hub uplinks via HaLow to base (~2 s @ 2 Mbps)
  T=80      All nodes back to deep sleep
